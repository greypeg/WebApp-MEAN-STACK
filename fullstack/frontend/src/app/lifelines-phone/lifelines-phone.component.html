Sabotage

Use this to instantly make your opponent's answers move around randomly

to make it hard for them to select the right answer. Get extra points if

your opponents fail to answer the question.


Pass

Use this to pass the current question without any cost. In the

end of the round you will not receive any points.

instead of four potential answers.


Ask the Audiance (TV - Show Only)

The player of the show will be able to see the audiance

distribution between each answer.


Phone a Friend (TV - Show Only)

The player of the show will be able to phone a friend

in order to help him answer the question.

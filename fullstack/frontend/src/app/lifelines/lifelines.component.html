Lifelines

Sabotage

Use this to instanlty make your opponent's answers move around randomly, making it hard for them to select the right answer. Get extra points if the opponents fail to answer the question.

Pass

Use this to pass the current question without losing any lives. In the end of the round you will not receive any points.

50-50 (TV - Show only)

When the player in the show use this lifeline, two of the potential answers gets instanlty removed , making it easier to find the right answer.

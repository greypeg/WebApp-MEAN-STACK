How To Play

In the Main Menu screen one player has to click in the “Start” button located at the top of the Main Menu. The players can connect to the game via their smartphones or tablets by putting a nickname and then clicking the “Join Live” button.

The players then are able to answer the questions appearing in their screen and the SurroundWall diplay. Answering questions correctly give players points and money which eventually will convert into points. The player with most points wins.

Collect points for every successful “Sabotage”, remaining lives and completing achievements like answering questions correctly while the audiance answered wrong.
